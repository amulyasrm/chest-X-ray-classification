The Road Ahead

What's Next for Med-AI? 🚀

While our current model is very strong, the world of medical AI is always moving forward. Here are the three main areas we are focusing on for the future:

1. Real-Time Deployment 📲

Instead of just running code, we want to build a mobile app where a doctor can take a photo of an X-ray film and get an instant AI analysis even in remote villages.

2. Explanable Reports ✍️

Currently, the AI gives a score. In the future, we want the AI to write a natural language report like: "A cloudy shadow is visible in the lower-left lung segment, suggesting early-stage pneumonia."

3. Multi-Modal Analysis 🧬

Imagine if the AI could look at the X-ray AND the patient's blood test results at the same time. This would make the diagnosis even more accurate.

Closing Summary 🏁

AI isn't here to replace doctors—it's here to empower them. By combining classic CNNs and modern Transformers, we are proving that smart technology can make healthcare faster, fairer, and more effective for everyone on the planet.

Thank you for exploring the future of medicine with us!
