The 4 Stages of Innovation

Our research journey wasn't a straight line. We evolved through four distinct pipelines, each one building on the failures and successes of the last.

Pipeline 1: The Standard Baseline

Legacy Phase

Setup & Goal

We started with CheXNet, the industry standard. It uses a DenseNet121 architecture which is very good at sharing information between its "brain cells" (layers).

  • Resolution: 224x224 (Standard)
  • Loss Function: Standard BCE
  • Strategy: Pure CNN approach.
Macro AUC: 0.843.
It was good, but it missed small features (like tiny nodules) because the image was too small and blurred.
graph TD In[X-ray 224px] --> CNN[DenseNet121] CNN --> Pool[Global Pool] Pool --> Out[15 Disease Preds]

Pipeline 2: The Hybrid Leap

Innovation Phase

The Upgrade ⚡

We introduced the CNN-Transformer Hybrid. We realized a CNN is like a microscope (sees textures), but a Transformer is like a map (sees locations). We combined them.

  • Upgrade: Added Swin Transformer branch.
  • Feature: Spatial Attention (Flashlight focus).
  • Experiment: Tested Focal Loss to handle rare diseases.
Macro AUC: 0.866.
A massive jump! The AI started understanding the "context" of where diseases sit in the lungs.
graph TD In[224px] --> CNN[CNN Branch] In --> Trans[Swin Branch] CNN --> Att[Attention] Att --> Fuse[Fusion] Trans --> Fuse Fuse --> Out[Predictions]

Pipeline 3: The High-Resolution Vision

Definition Phase

The Upgrade ⚡

Resolution matters. In medical imaging, zooming in can save lives. We scaled everything from 224px to 384x384px.

  • Upgrade: 3x more pixels than Pipeline 2.
  • Feature: Swin Window-12 Scaling to handle larger patches.
  • Benefit: Tiny diseases (Nodules) became visible to the AI.
Macro AUC: 0.869.
Precision improved, but we noticed a "Data Leakage" problem where the AI was memorizing certain patients.
graph TD In3[384px HD] --> H[Hybrid Model] H --> FL[Focal Loss] FL --> Score[High Precision Score]

Pipeline 4: Optimized Mastery (Final)

Gold Standard 🏆

The Final Upgrade ⚡

We fixed the data integrity. We implemented Patient-wise Splitting—ensuring the AI never sees the same person in training and testing. We also stabilized the math using optimized BCE loss.

  • Final Fix: No Patient Leakage allowed.
  • Final Loss: Optimized BCEWithLogits.
  • Efficiency: MPS Memory Caching for fast inference.
Macro AUC: 0.884
This is our most trustworthy and accurate model to date.
