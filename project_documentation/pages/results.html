Performance Benchmark 📈

How do we know our AI is actually "smart"? We use a score called AUC.
A 1.0 is a perfect score (like a psychic model), and a 0.5 is like guessing with a coin flip.

Model Macro AUC (Mean Score) Reliability Status
CheXNet (Baseline) 0.843 Average
Novel Hybrid (224) 0.866 Advanced
Novel Hybrid (384) 0.869 High-Precision
Optimized Hybrid (384) 0.884 State-of-the-Art 🏆

Our Optimized Hybrid model reached a score of 0.884, which is extremely high and shows it's consistent across multiple lung conditions.

Visualization: Grad-CAM 🔍

One big problem with AI is that it's often a "Black Box"—we don't know why it chose a certain answer. With Grad-CAM, we can see exactly where the AI is looking!

The "Heatmap" View

AI Looking at... RED REGIONS = Pathological shadow
If the AI says "Pneumonia" but its Heatmap shows it was looking at the patient's shoulder bone, we know the AI is wrong. But if the Heatmap highlights the bottom of the lungs exactly where the doctor sees an infection, we can Trust the AI.
