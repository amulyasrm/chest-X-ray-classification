Random Rotation (10 Degrees)

Random Horizontal Flip

Flips the image across the vertical axis, creating a mirror image to help the model learn lateral lung symmetry without distorting organ shapes.

Color Jitter (Brightness Adjustment)

Randomly brightens or darkens the image by 10% to simulate X-Rays taken with slightly different exposure strengths.

Harsh Augmentation Methods

(Extreme distortions avoided in our pipeline to preserve anatomy)

6

Random Erasing (Cutout)

Randomly blacks out a rectangular section of the image to force the model to look at the entire lung instead of memorizing one tiny feature.

Original X-Ray

After Random Erasing

7

Solarize

Inverts all pixel colors that are above a certain brightness threshold, creating a high-contrast, photo-negative appearance.

Original X-Ray

After Solarize

8

Elastic Transform

Warps and stretches the image as if it were printed on rubber, severely distorting the anatomical structure of the ribs and heart.

Original X-Ray

After Elastic Transform

9

Gaussian Noise

Sprinkles random static "grain" across the entire image to simulate severe sensor noise or ultra-low quality X-ray machines.
