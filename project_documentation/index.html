Revolutionizing Radiology with Hybrid Deep Learning

A multi-phase ablation study on NIH ChestX-ray14 dataset using CNN-Transformer Fusion, high-resolution scaling, and optimized loss strategies.

Project Objectives

Clinical Accuracy

Develop a robust multi-label classifier capable of detecting 14 distinct pathologies with State-of-the-Art (SOTA) AUC scores.

Hybrid Architecture

Fuse local spatial features from CNNs with global context from Vision Transformers to capture complex disease patterns.

Ablation Study

Conduct a systematic study on the impact of image resolution (224 vs 384) and architectural enhancements on performance.
