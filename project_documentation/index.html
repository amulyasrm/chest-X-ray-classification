A Research Journey

Hybrid CNN-Transformer for Chest X-ray Diagnosis

Teaching AI to read X-rays like a doctor. Discover how we combined two powerful AI brains to help detect 14 types of diseases.

The Goal 🎯

Our mission is simple but powerful: To build an AI assistant that can accurately find diseases in Chest X-rays. In many parts of the world, there aren't enough expert doctors (radiologists) to look at every X-ray. An AI can act as a second pair of eyes, working 24/7 to help doctors catch diseases early.

Why Chest X-rays? 🫁

Chest X-rays are the most common medical images used to diagnose problems with the lungs and heart. From Pneumonia to Lung Cancer, the first step in saving a life is often a clear X-ray diagnosis.

Looking for a disease in an X-ray is like finding a specific "faint gray smudge" in a room full of gray clouds. It's really hard, and even experts spend years learning how to do it.

The Big Reveal: Our Results 📈

After months of testing different AI architectures, we built a Hybrid Model. Instead of using just one method, we combined the "spatial precision" of CNNs with the "global understanding" of Transformers.
